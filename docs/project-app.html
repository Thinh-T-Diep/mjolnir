Team Project Idea

Anlen Fridge - App

  • Summary
  • Motivation
  • Hardware
  • Software
  • App

We will also make an app to go with the smart fridge to utilize all of its technology. The app will be compatible on all operating system. At first, we wanted to develop a simple protype “usable” app with very basic functions. But after careful reconsideration, we decided to stop at the sketches for the app UI only because we don’t have enough time nor the resources to make an app that will be up to our expectation. All of the decent app maker online like Appmkr or Appy Pie cost money and the free application like MIT app inventor is too basic and won’t be able to fully express our vision and ideas. That’s why we decided to make the sketches only so that we will have more time to focus on its functionality and design a nice and clean interface. So, this is just at the very beginning stage of developing an app.

Live cam

The second function on the tool bar is live cam. It’ll show the users what’s in their fridge and also, other information on the small note box aside, such as the date, the date the user last bought his groceries and expected expiry for a few products. The smart diagnosis below will also give diet suggestion for user based on the food in their fridge.

Settings

Setting is the last function of the tool bar, which allows users to modify the temperature of the fridge, camera setting, power-saving mode, etc. the whole idea is to provide custom modifications for customer.

Shopping List

The last function is the shopping list. The shopping list keeps record of what the customer needs to buy. Based on the expired list and the user can choose whether to put the item in the shopping list. It will also link with the fridge to generate suggestions based on their diet and preferences to a better diet plan that is both nutritious and optimal for customer.
